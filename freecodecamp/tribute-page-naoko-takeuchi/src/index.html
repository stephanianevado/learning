Who is Naoko Takeuchi?

Is a Japanese manga artist. She is best known as the author of Sailor Moon, one of the most popular manga series of all time.

Let's see a line time of her life:

  • 1967 - Born in K≈çfu, Yamanashi, Japan.
  • 1973 - 1982 - She attended Kofu Ichi High School. She wore sailor suit uniforms and joined the astronomy and manga clubs. These experiences influenced her work for Sailor Moon, in addition to her other pieces such as Love Call and Rain Kiss.
  • 1985 - She worked as a miko at the Shiba Daijingu shrine near her university. This experience later became the basis for one of her characters in Sailor Moon, Rei Hino.
  • 1986 - She graduated from Kyoritsu University of Pharmacy, where she received a degree in chemistry and became a licensed pharmacist. Her senior thesis was called "Heightened Effects of Thrombolytic Actions Due to Ultrasound".
  • 1987 - 1989 - She entered the manga industry by submitting to Kodansha her work Love Call and starts writing Maria.
  • 1990 - 1991 - She published Maria and then she worked on the ice-skating series The Cherry Project. She wanted to do a manga on outer space and girl fighters. This concept would later become a one-shot called Sailor V, which would later begin serialization in RunRun. When Toei Animation planned to adapt her manga into an anime series, she reworked Sailor V and added four other superheroines.
  • 1991 - 1997 - In December she began serializing Sailor Moon, which became an instant hit. The success encouraged her to work on both Sailor Moon and Sailor V from 1991 to 1997 until it was canceled. During that 6-year period, she produced 52 chapters that were collected in 18 volumes. The success of the manga led to a 200-episode anime adaptation, three animated films, numerous video games, and wide-ranging merchandising.
  • 1998 - Her first appearance in the United States at the San Diego Comic-Con International convention. Also she published the first Sailor Moon Infinity Collection Art Book with limited releases.
  • 1999 - She married to Yoshihiro Togashi, the author of Yu Yu Hakusho and Hunter x Hunter. From this marriage, the couple have two children: a son, born in January 2001, and another child, born in 2009.
  • 2003 - 2004 - She started producing Pretty Guardian Sailor Moon and continued to work on Toki Meca.
  • 2012 - It was announced that a new Sailor Moon anime adaptation was in development.
  • 2014 - 2016 - The new reboot anime debuted on July of 2014, titled Pretty Guardian Sailor Moon Crystal. It streamed on Niconico bi-weekly, and ended on July 2015, total of 26 episodes, covering the Dark Kingdom and Black Moon arc of the manga. The series eventually gained its 13-episode third season, covering the Infinity arc of the manga (subtitled Death Busters), and aired weekly on Japanese television from April to June 2016.
  • 2020 - present - In 2020, was relased the two volumes "All Colored Eternal Edition" of the Sailor Moon manga. In 2021, she supervised the production of the two-part anime film, Pretty Guardian Sailor Moon Eternal: The Movie, which adapted the Dream arc of the manga (subtitled Dead Moon), and acted as a "fourth season" to the Sailor Moon Crystal series. Both films were released in 2021 in Japanese theaters.
