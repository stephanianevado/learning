Nutrinevado

Is a nutrition platform, created by Stephania Nevado. Integrative nutrition for all body types.

How it works:

  1. Choose the plan that best suits your preferences.
  2. You will receive an email after having made the payment, fill in the form so that i can meet you. I do this part in writing because we think that writing is a necessary and healing activity.
  3. I will contact you by mail to have the 1st session of 45-60 min in depth. I want to know you, I want to know your preferences, your lifestyle, your daily activity, your history, your emotions, your history with diets, everything.
  4. After 5-7 days you will receive the guideline and all the resources to help you on this path.
  5. In a few days or weeks after receiving the documents, I will be able to evaluate how long to meet in a 2nd session to be able to see how you feel, resolve doubts and talk about possible obstacles.
  6. You will have contact by email or by whatsapp to resolve all your questions and comments.
  7. I follow up depending on your process and your individual needs (video calls for example). I will continue to solve your everyday challenges, and work together to solve them.
